Welcome to EcoWise

This platform empowers citizens to recycle responsibly, connect with local NGOs, and learn about sustainable waste management—all through an agentic AI assistant. EcoWise is designed for simplicity and accessibility, enabling users from all backgrounds to participate in building cleaner, smarter cities. Whether you're unsure how to dispose of a specific item, want to volunteer, or are looking for reliable information on waste segregation, EcoWise guides you every step of the way. By integrating AI into everyday sustainability practices, we bridge the gap between awareness and action.

Our Purpose

We aim to build sustainable cities by simplifying how people interact with recycling systems. Our AI helps users classify waste, locate recycling centers, and stay informed. But more than that, our purpose is to inspire behavior change and empower communities with tools to take action. Through education and localized guidance, we support citizens, schools, businesses, and NGOs in developing better waste habits. EcoWise is not just about technology—it’s about creating a shared culture of responsibility, where everyone plays a role in shaping a sustainable urban future..

Impact of Waste on Our Planet

Unmanaged waste contributes to pollution, health hazards, and climate change. Sustainable handling can reduce our carbon footprint and create a cleaner future. When waste is not properly sorted or processed, it often ends up in landfills or oceans, damaging ecosystems and threatening wildlife. Toxic materials can seep into the soil and water, affecting food chains and human health. Burning waste contributes to air pollution and greenhouse gases. However, by embracing recycling, composting, and mindful consumption, we can dramatically cut down on waste generation, conserve natural resources, and promote environmental resilience.
